FIND WORK HERE!

SecondChance Indy teams up with Indianapolis' premier staffing agencies and employers to fill job placements. All jobs listed in our portal are available for reentering citizens.

WE WANT TO HELP YOU.

Welcome back to our community, and we’re so glad that you visited our website. Our services are available to you at no charge. We hope we can connect you with the job opportunity that’s right for you.

HOW WE CAN HELP.

Instead of becoming discouraged while searching for employers willing to consider hiring someone with a criminal record, here (below left) you can search for jobs available in the Indianapolis metropolitan area from employers who are definitely willing to give second chances.

YOU MATTER TO US.

In addition to a good job, we know that you have many other needs in your effort to reestablish yourself. Our Resource list (below right) provides links to websites with an incredibly wide range of services available to you. If you don’t find what you need the first time, visit us again, because we’re adding more services every day.

JOBS AVAILABLE

We’ve compiled a list of jobs available from second chance employers in Central Indiana (Counties of Marion, Hamilton, Boone, Hendricks, Morgan, Johnson, Shelby, Hancock, and Madison). You can search the entire list as well as by type of job or by the distance from your home. Start your job search NOW!

RESOURCES

In addition to a good job, you’ll need access to housing, food, identification, clothing, healthcare, and a host of other services, all of which can be found on our website. We also frequently update our website with new information everyday on the latest news and services. Find out what you need NOW!

MISSION & VISION

Mission: To immediately connect those reentering our community from incarceration with employers willing to provide second chances.

Vision: All reentering our community from incarceration are able to find good jobs and other resources that enable them to support themselves, their families and to become good neighbors and citizens.
